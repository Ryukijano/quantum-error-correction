# Code of Conduct

Syndrome-Net is a technical community focused on reproducible quantum-error-correction research.

## Community standards

- Be respectful and constructive in all discussions.
- Critique ideas and evidence, not people.
- Keep communication focused, actionable, and evidence-driven.
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, or demeaning language.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Personal attacks, threats, or intimidation.
- Repeated disruptive behavior after a request to stop.
- Public posting of private details without consent.
- Spam, trolling, or deliberate misrepresentation of project artifacts.

## Scope and reporting

These expectations apply to issues, PRs, and project-related communication channels.
If you experience a violation, report it to repository maintainers for review.
